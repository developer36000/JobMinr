We’re looking for an experienced UI Designer to join our team in Canada. As a designer at New Haircut, you will need to create visually appealing interactive prototypes for web & mobile digital products. You’ll be working side by side with the client, product managers, engineers, researchers, and other designers.

Skills:

  • php
  • UX
  • UI

Qualifications:

Demonstrable UI design skills with a strong portfolio
+3 year experience in web and/or mobile applications design
Strong talent for visual design, layout, and typography with excellent attention to detail
Proficiency in tools such as Sketch, InVision, Photoshop & Illustrator
Communication: strong writing and presentation skills
Problem Solving: ability to solve problems creatively and effectively
Mindset: a clear understanding of the importance of user-centered design; willingness to seek and receive feedback; ability to self-manage and autonomy
Up-to-date with the latest design trends, techniques, and best practices.
Proficient in English
Willing to travel internationally:20%

Responsibilities:

Create & sketch ideas, designs, storyboards, user flows
Create consistent, modern, and visually appealing brand and style guides
Build low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes that effectively communicate interaction and concept
Design consistently and appropriately across browsers nd devices
Perform user testing with the product in question & document results
Collaborate closely with developers and UX designers
Present concepts and designs to clients & internal teams and gather additional feedback and direction
Continuously research design and technology trends, and understand when it’s appropriate to use them or forge new paths
Nice to have:
Front-end skills
Brand / Illustration skills
